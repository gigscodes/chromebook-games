<<<<< Tutorial of How to Download >>>>>

1) Download Chrome Games on another device ( not chromebook )
2) Login into your school account on your device and click the 9 dots in your browser
3) Open drives and drag Chrome Games into it
4) Get your Chromebook and click the magnifying glass on your keyboard
5) Click files and open drive
6) Find Chrome Games and drag it into downloads for it to work
